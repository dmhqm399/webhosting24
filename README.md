## Who Even Is Webhosting24?

Founded by Thomas Moroder and headquartered in Ortisei, South Tyrol (Italy), Webhosting24 is the kind of small-team provider where the founder's name still shows up in testimonials. They're an ICANN-accredited domain registrar, a RIPE NCC member, and an APNIC member — which is just a fancy way of saying they're plugged into the global internet infrastructure in ways that genuinely matter for reliability.

Their pitch is simple: DIY hosting without the DIY headaches. You get a real cPanel, real backups, and support via phone, ticket, or live chat. No dark patterns during checkout. No surprise "you need to buy the premium tier to get basic features" nonsense.

The company operates out of Europe (Munich datacenter for VPS), but also has NVMe VPS nodes in Singapore, Tokyo, Sydney, Los Angeles, New York, and Miami — so if you're targeting global audiences, you've got actual geographic choices.
